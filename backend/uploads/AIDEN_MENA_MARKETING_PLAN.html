AIDEN - MENA Region Marketing Plan

AI-Powered Customer Support Platform

Prepared for: ZAIA Systems | Date: December 2024

Executive Summary

Aiden is an AI-powered customer support chatbot platform that enables businesses to deploy intelligent, multilingual chatbots trained on their own knowledge base.

Key Value Propositions:
  • 90% cost reduction vs. traditional support teams
  • 24/7 availability in Arabic and English
  • No-code setup in under 30 minutes
  • Enterprise-grade security and data privacy
  • Seamless human handoff when needed
